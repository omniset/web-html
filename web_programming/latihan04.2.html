Low and Slow

This week I amextremelyexcited about a new cooking technique calledsous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to the target temperature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book Cooking for Geeks, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low- temperature poaching.
